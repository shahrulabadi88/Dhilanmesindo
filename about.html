Dhilanmesindo is a company founded in Batu City - Malang with the main objective of increasing the economic value of local agricultural products, especially apples into innovative and quality products. Since 1975 we have been planting apples in the family garden located on the slopes of Mount Arjuna, Batu city - Malang. Local wisdom and noble values are reflected in every product we create.

"Local Brand Become Global Brand"

Our motto is to create goodness to introduce our products going to global.

Apel Celup are apples that we dry, we process into apple tea. We use local apples, namely manalagi and romebeauty apples which are abundant in Batu City.
